Who is Dolly?

Dolly Parton, is an American singer, songwriter, actress, businesswoman, and philanthropist, has had a truly remarkable and diverse career. She was born in 1946 in Tennessee and became famous for her unique voice and hit songs like "Jolene" and "I Will Always Love You." She also found success in movies such as "9 to 5" and "Steel Magnolias."

She is not only a talented artist but also a businesswoman with successful ventures in music publishing, film production, and owning a theme park. Additionally, she is dedicated to giving back through initiatives like the Imagination Library, which provides free books to children. Dolly's impact across various industries has made her a beloved and iconic figure.
